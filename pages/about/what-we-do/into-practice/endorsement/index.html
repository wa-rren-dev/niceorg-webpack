Endorsement

Do you have a tool or resource that you would like NICE to endorse? 

How to start: 

  1. Has your resource been designed to support the implementation of our guidance or quality standards?  

    If yes, then: 

  2. Check that you meet our criteria for endorsement.
  3. Contact our endorsement team, to express an interest in applying.  We will discuss your potential application with you.
  4. If we have confirmed that you can apply, please complete this application form.
  5. Send the form to the endorsement team.

Read the full endorsement process manual
